Our Mission

To craft seamless, personalized, and memorable city-to-city travel experiences across Ethiopia's most incredible cities, landscapes, and cultures.

A Short History

Founded in 2015 in Addis Ababa, City2City Travel began as a boutique agency focused on connecting Ethiopian cities with unique cultural and scenic experiences. Inspired by the diversity of Ethiopia—from the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the shores of Lake Tana and the bustling streets of Addis Ababa—City2City Travel offers seamless city-to-city itineraries. Over the years, we expanded to include guided tours across the country, ensuring authentic experiences while maintaining our commitment to quality, reliability, and exceptional service.
